Lemonade Recipe

Description

A simple recipe for a classic drink.

Ingredients

  • Water
  • Lemons
  • Sugar
  • Ice

Steps

  1. To allow the sugar to dissolve, heat up water and add sugar to create a syrup
  2. Make lemon juice
  3. Add the 2 liquids together, using a ratio that suits your palate
  4. Add ice to chill the drink. Enjoy!
